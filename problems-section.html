THE PROBLEMS
05

Five ways deals were dying before they started.

01 Foundational

No Digital Presence

Buyers search online first — if you're not there, you're invisible. Competitors with basic sites were absorbing every lead before you even knew they existed.

02 Urgent

Lost Leads on WhatsApp

Leads message at 2 AM. By 9 AM they've already contacted a competitor. No tracking, no follow-up system.

03 Systemic

No CRM

Client data scattered across registers, phones, and chats. No way to know which leads are hot or where revenue is leaking.

04 Operational

Document Chaos

Agreements, KYC, and property papers scattered across WhatsApp groups. Finding one document takes hours.

05 Recurring

Manual Follow-ups

Site visit reminders were manual. Re-engaging old leads was impossible. Good opportunities slipped through every week.
